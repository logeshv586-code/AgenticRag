Welcome to Era Timbers
At Era Timbers, we have been dedicated to providing high-quality timber products at affordable price of a common man since our establishment in 2013. Having its HQ at Chennai, TN, India, ERA Timbers has widespread processing sawmills in Chennai, Malaysia and Africa. With decades of experience in the timber industry, we specialize in sourcing, processing, and supplying premium wood materials that meet the diverse needs of construction, furniture making, and other wood-based industries.
Focused on all Timber and its allied products, currently our facility is processing around 250 cubic metres of Timber per month with its vision to become one of the leading suppliers across the nation. Having established a strong presence in the plywood, hardware and interior products industry, Era Timbers has become a one-stop destination for all timber and interior requirements.
